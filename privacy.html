Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 27, 2025

Overview

At ERA Intelligence, we believe privacy is a fundamental right. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application and services.

We never sell personal information. All data is anonymized before leaving your device.

Information We Collect

Voice Data

When you participate in voice collection activities:

  • Short audio recordings (typically 30 seconds) of your voice
  • Audio quality metrics and technical specifications
  • General recording environment information (indoor/outdoor, noise level)

Network Data

Our app automatically collects network performance information:

  • Signal strength and network type (4G, 5G, WiFi)
  • Internet speed measurements
  • General location data (city-level, not precise GPS)
  • Device type and operating system version

Account Information

  • Email address for account creation and payments
  • Payment information (processed securely through PayPal)
  • App usage statistics and preferences

How We Use Your Data

Voice Samples

Your voice recordings help improve:

  • AI speech recognition systems
  • Voice assistants and smart speakers
  • Accessibility tools for people with disabilities
  • Language learning applications

Network Data

Network information is used to:

  • Help telecommunications companies improve coverage
  • Optimize network performance in your area
  • Plan infrastructure improvements
  • Research wireless technology development

Data Protection & Anonymization

Before Data Leaves Your Device

  • All personal identifiers are removed
  • Voice recordings are processed to remove background conversations
  • Location data is generalized to city or region level
  • Data is encrypted using industry-standard protocols

What We DON'T Collect

  • Your precise GPS location
  • Contact lists or personal files
  • Text messages or call logs
  • Social media information
  • Browsing history

Your Rights & Controls

You Can Always:

  • Delete your data: Request removal of all your contributions
  • Control collection: Choose which types of data to share
  • Pause anytime: Stop data collection whenever you want
  • Export your data: Get a copy of your account information
  • Close your account: Delete everything and stop all collection

You maintain full control over your data participation at all times.

Data Sharing

Who We Share With:

  • Technology companies building AI systems (anonymized voice data)
  • Telecommunications companies (anonymized network data)
  • Research institutions (aggregated, anonymized datasets)

We NEVER Share:

  • Personal information or contact details
  • Raw, unprocessed recordings
  • Data that can be traced back to you
  • Information with advertisers or marketers

Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure cloud storage with enterprise-grade protection
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Limited access controls for our team members
  • Automatic data deletion after processing
