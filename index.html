Frequently Asked Questions

Is my personal information safe?
Yes, all data is anonymized before leaving your device. We never collect personal information, and you can delete your data at any time through the app settings.
How much can I realistically earn per month?
Most users earn between $15-45 per month, depending on their activity level and local demand for data. Voice samples pay more than network data, so active participation increases earnings.
What happens to my voice recordings?
Voice recordings are used to train AI speech recognition systems. They are anonymized, encrypted, and sold to technology companies working on voice assistants, transcription services, and accessibility tools.
Does the app drain my phone's battery?
ERA Intelligence is optimized for minimal battery usage. Network monitoring uses less than 2% of daily battery life, and voice recording only occurs when you actively participate.
When and how do I get paid?
Payments are sent automatically every Friday via PayPal. There's no minimum payout threshold, so you'll receive money for any amount earned during the week.
