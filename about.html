About ERA Intelligence

Building the future of ethical data collection, one contribution at a time.

Our Founder

ERA Intelligence was founded by a college sophomore studying Cybersecurity and Computer Science with a passion for ethical technology. Driven by the belief that data collection should be transparent and mutually beneficial, our founder is dedicated to making a positive difference in the world through innovative solutions.

Our Mission

We believe in making the world better through technology. ERA Intelligence was born from the idea that individuals should be fairly compensated for their valuable data contributions while maintaining complete privacy and control over their information.

Our Vision

To create a future where data collection is transparent, ethical, and mutually beneficial. We're building technology that respects user privacy while enabling breakthrough innovations in AI and telecommunications.
